SiteScan

About project

SiteScan is a platform designed to evaluate websites for potential fraud and assess their risk scores. By analyzing various factors, such as domain age or security measures implemented, SiteScan provides a comprehensive risk assessment for online platforms. Users can easily check the credibility of a website before engaging, ensuring safer browsing experiences. The intuitive interface allows for quick and efficient scans, while detailed reports offer insights into possible fraud indicators. With SiteScan, you can navigate the web with confidence, making informed decisions about where to spend your time and money online.
